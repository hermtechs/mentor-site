Why This Program ?

Cyber Security Mentoring

Have a good understanding of IT and security? Maybe you have a CompTIA Security+ or other IT cert or have been studying but you just want an understanding of the different tools and what employers are looking for you to know to enter a cyber security role? We can assist!

We can walk you through the various tools that cyber security analysts use and specific the most popular vendor products that employers would like you to now and understand how to use.

Resume coaching and building

Believe it or not, many people including you may have the necessary skills to work your dream job, but your resume may not reflect it. In today’s world what makes you competitive is a resume with the right keywords and phrases on it.

We can help you accomplish this as well as show you what strategies will make you appear at the top of search results when employers are looking for candidates.

Overemployed (Job Polygamy) Coaching

Want to double or triple what you can make? This coaching is for you. We will go over the proper setup for you to be overemployed and working more than one remote job simultaneously.

We will also show you ways that you can heavily reduce the risk of an employer noticing. And ways, equipment, and strategies of how to remain productive at more than one job at once and appear productive. As such this can pave the way for an early retirement.

Digital Nomad & Living Abroad

Already have a job and want to find out the best way to work it from abroad? Want to know the best way to work it abroad while still appearing as if you are working from the U.S. or wherever you home country is as well as reducing your monthly expenses?

We can guide you on how to accomplish this. Also, would you like to get a residency permit in another country for tax purposes? We can also assist you with that so you can establish a foreign bank account and claim the coveted bona fide residence on your federal taxes. Lastly, we can talk also about how to get an apartment long term or housing in whichever country you decide to choose.
